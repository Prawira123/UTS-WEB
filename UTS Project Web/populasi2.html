Unique White Tiger


The white tiger is a very rare animal because of the color of its fur those that depend on recessive genes passed down from their parents. Reported by AZ Animals, white tigers are becoming increasingly rare in the wild due to poaching or capture for the exotic pet trade. The white tiger is a rare group of tigers from the Bengal tiger species (Panthera tigris tigris).
This subspecies is found in India, Bangladesh, Nepal, and Bhutan. Today there are hundreds of white tigers in captivity around the world, about a hundred from India and the number is growing.
