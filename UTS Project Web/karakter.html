CHARACTERISTIC

Of The Exotic Animal


White tigers are very rare animals because their coat color depends on a recessive gene passed down from their parents. this is characteristic of White Tiger
pink nose, center pink feet, dark gray skin, icy blue eyes, and white or yellow-white fur with black, gray or brown stripes.
